Plot: a spin-off of th series "The Big Bang Theory" that shows Sheldon's life in East Texas, beginning from when he starts high school at age 9.

Episodes: 65 (in 3 seasons), about 20 minutes each

My opinion: as a super-fan of the main series, “The Big Bang Theory”, I couldn’t help but watch also this spin-off.
I loved it! Sheldon is one of the most iconic characters ever, and I needed to know more about his past in the East Texas.
Everything fits with what is shown in the main series and there are so many references to the future Sheldon (I LOVED IT!). Moreover, it hints not-shown facts of Sheldon’s future and it explains so many things hinted in “The Big Bang Theory”.
At the beginning I was only interested in Sheldon: his siblings are mentioned few times and the “The Big Bang Theory” Mary is unpleasant. But by the end of the first season I loved also Missy and George jr. and all the other characters. I mean¬¬¬¬– how couldn’t I?!?!?
It showed how Sheldon’s family really was and how was his relationship with it (despite all that adult Sheldon says, he really loves his sister Missy and his father; his parents’ marriage wasn’t so troublesome, after all; and no one could have expected how «Meemaw» really is).
Now that I finished it, I feel kind of sad, now I have nothing more related to “The Big Bang Theory”, and I guess I have to let it go. But I will miss it, and also the actors and the characters.
And how it finished!!!!! I can’t wait for the fourth season to be released, and because of the pandemic there isn’t a defined date yet.
But watch “The Big Bang Theory” first, I know it’s long, but it’s worth it. Plus, if you watch “Young Sheldon” first, you’d miss a lot of details and you wouldn’t understand all the characters.

Spoiler alert: can we just talk about how good this series is?
It explained how “bazinga!” was born, George jr. buisness, Sheldon’s friendship with Tam, how Sheldon started reading comics… So many things.
But the end of season 2?!?!? I was heartbroken.
And when Sheldon and his father go to Pasadena and visit the canteen of CalTech?!?!? I was dying.
Anyway, I need to know how Sheldon will go to college and all the things that were left unanswered about George jr..
